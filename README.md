## Key Findings

- Dutch audio market valued at **€480M** with 7.2% CAGR (2024–2028)
- **68% of electronics** purchased online in the Netherlands — ideal for D2C entry
- boAt's sweet spot: **€45–€75 tier**, underpenetrated between JBL (€60–€180) and Jabra (€90–€300)
- Year 1 revenue target: **€1.8M** (~30,000 units at avg €60)
- Break-even projected at **Month 9** (~12,500 units)
